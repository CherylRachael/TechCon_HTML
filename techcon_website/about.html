Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots meetup for passionate developers and quickly grew into one of the most influential technology conferences in the world. Over the years, TechCon has welcomed thousands of attendees, showcasing breakthrough innovations and fostering a collaborative community.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to connect innovators, creators, and leaders who are shaping the future of technology. Our mission is to foster open dialogue, encourage bold ideas, and provide a platform where diverse voices from all corners of the industry can be heard and celebrated.
